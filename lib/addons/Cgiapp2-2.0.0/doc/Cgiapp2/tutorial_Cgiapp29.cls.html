Credits

Thank you to Jesse Erlbaum for reviewing the initial release of this class and approving my release of it to the greater PHP programming community. Thank you as well to the CGI::Application mailing lists for many tips and pointers (though I've only been lurking), and to the CGI::Application development team for continuing work on that excellent perl module.

Several people have greatly improved my understanding of PHP, design patterns, and PHP's OOP model. Thanks go in particular to Paul M. Jones and Mike Naberezny.

Thanks also go to my former employer, the National Gardening Association, for allowing me the time to port CGI::Application to PHP in order to make my work easier; in particular, many thanks to my then-IT Manager, Rob Riggen, for his support.
