Eligibility

Please refer to the University of Melbourne's Branding Policy (MPF1193) for details about staff and student eligibility for purchase of University of Melbourne branded stationery.

Approval

  • Clients are able to download a low-res, watermarked PDF of the purpose of proofing artwork.
  • No job will go into production unless submitted to print by the client.

Important: The client is ultimately responsible for final approval of all artwork submitted to print production via this system. Submitting to print is deemed as approval of artwork and authorisation to proceed with the order. Submitting to print is also deemed as confirmation that the client has followed any and all approval processes as required by their organisational unit and that the information presented on the stationery being ordered is in accordance with University of Melbourne's Branding Policy (MPF1193).

Design, Print & Merchandise (DPM) and the print supplier accept no responsibility for typographical errors, spelling mistakes or incorrect information on any item. No refunds or reprints are given after a final approved design has gone to print due to oversights by the client's proof reading. Reprints will be charged at full price.

Pricing

A price will be advised when you configure your order.

Turnaround and delivery

The stationery is printed once a week in order to obtain the best combination of economy and time. Orders must be approved by 2:00 PM Thursday for delivery to External Relations on the following Tuesday afternoon. Stationery can be collected from External Relations at level 2, 32 Lincoln Square North, or can be despatched through the University's internal mail system. External Relations can take no responsibility for delivery once the item/s leaves our office.

Stationery considerations

Please note that stationery items, particularly business cards can only hold a limited amount of information and should ideally contain essential contact information only.

Some formats may not supported by the online system. This includes the following:

  • items that have multiple contact numbers
  • items that may feature a previously approved secondary logo (co-branded) or where a template does not presently exist
  • BELIEVE campaign related stationery (please see BELIEVE campaign section below)

For formats that cannot be ordered online, please complete and submit the Offline Stationery Order Form.
