University branded business cards, letterheads and with compliments slips can be ordered here. This is an external service operated by McKellar Renown Press, although invoices will be issued internally via a charge out from the Marketing Office.

Using the system

Ordering stationery is a simple 3-step process.

  1. Select stationery type
  2. Prepare artwork
  3. Submit order

Eligibility

Please refer to the University of Melbourne's Branding Policy (MPF1193) for details about staff and student eligibility for purchase of University of Melbourne branded stationery.

Approval

  • Clients are able to download a low-res, watermarked PDF of the purpose of proofing artwork.
  • No job will go into production unless submitted to print by the client.

Important: The client is ultimately responsible for final approval of all artwork submitted to print production via this system. Submitting to print is deemed as approval of artwork and authorisation to proceed with the order. Submitting to print is also deemed as confirmation that the client has followed any and all approval processes as required by their organisational unit and that the information presented on the stationery being ordered is in accordance with University of Melbourne's Branding Policy (MPF1193).

University Marketing and the print supplier accept no responsibility for typographical errors, spelling mistakes or incorrect information on any item. No refunds or reprints are given after a final approved design has gone to print due to oversights by the client's proof reading. Reprints will be charged at full price.


Pricing

A price will be advised when you configure your order.

Turnaround and delivery

The stationery is printed once a week in order to obtain the best combination of economy and time. Orders must be approved by 3:00 PM Thursday for delivery to the Marketing Office on Tuesday afternoon. Stationery can be collected from Marketing Office at level 3/780 Elizabeth St, or can be despatched through the University's internal mail system, however Marketing takes no responsibility for the delivery once it leaves the Marketing Office.

Stationery considerations

Please note that stationery items, particularly business cards can only hold a limited amount of information and should ideally contain essential contact information only.

For formats that cannot be ordered online, please complete and submit the Requisition for University Stationery Order Form - via email to Winnie Au Yeung.

To order stationery supporting "Believe: The Campaign for The University of Melbourne", please complete and submit the Requisition for Believe Stationery Order Form - via email to Winnie Au Yeung.
